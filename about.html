About this Dollmaker

This is a dollmaker for creating sprites for use with Renpy and other visual novel engines, or just for fun. It is currently in alpha. Saves made now will definitely break in later releases.

Please let me know if you hit any bugs or if there's any extra features you'd like, though I make no guarantees. If the spritemaker isn't working at all, try reloading the page.

Known Bugs

  • Occasional flicker while image is updating
  • Colours sometimes take a second or two to 'catch up' to the right value.
  • Import and right-click-save don't work on all browsers. Chrome and Firefox seem to be ok.
  • Colour picker is sometimes slightly off.
  • Randomly generated hairstyles do not combine types of hair properly.
